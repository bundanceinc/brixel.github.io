Meet Brixels, the most relaxing block puzzle game

Brixels is an indie game focused on helping you relax while playing it. The game purposely has a simple interface and reduced animations, it's meant to feel meditative.

The game includes powers that allow you to get out of tough spots, but they're not required for you to enjoy or succeed in the game.
